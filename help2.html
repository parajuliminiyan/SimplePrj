Help Center (FAQ)

What is hamrokotha.com ?
-> Hamrokotha.com is an online platform that connects two users the Ad Poster & The Client(one who needs Room/Flat).

What is the Main aim of hamrokotha.com ?
-> Actually we aim at providing the Home to needy ones & a source to Earn for the one who post to our site.

How does hamrokotha.com works ?
-> A agreement is made in between Client & Poster, if Client gets the Room/Flat posted by the Poster then Client must pay to the Poster.

How can I find Room/Flat ?
-> Simply Login to Hamrokotha.com and search through the location you have dreamt of living.

How can I Earn ?
-> Simply post about the Room/flat nearby you and get paid by the Client one who contacts you

Who are the Client ?
-> Anyone who seeks for Room/Flat in Hamrokotha.com are our Client.

Who are the Poster ?
-> Anyone who post the information about vacant Room/Flats in Hamrokotha.com are the Poster.

Is it compulsory to upload Image while Posting ?
-> We consider image to be Mandatory thing while Posting on Hamrokotha.com .

Can i be Poster & Client at the same time ?
-> Yes, you can be both at the same time.
